About Us

Technex Robotics And STEM EDU is a forward-thinking educational company focused on delivering high-quality Robotics and STEM training programs in schools. We partner with educational institutions to provide skilled trainers, structured curriculum, and hands-on learning experiences that prepare students for future technologies. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional education and modern innovation by making robotics and STEM accessible, practical, and engaging for every student.

Our Services

Robotics Training

Hands-on robotics sessions with real-world applications, enabling students to build, program, and innovate.

STEM Education

Interactive STEM curriculum designed to enhance problem-solving, creativity, and analytical thinking.

School Partnerships

End-to-end collaboration with schools including trainer deployment, curriculum support, and program management.
